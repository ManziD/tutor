How lessons run

A fixed sequence, because exam preparation isn't guesswork.

The order matters — each stage exists for a reason.

01 — diagnose

Free diagnostic lesson

Before we start, I sit with the student's last three tests or a past paper to find exactly which topics need to be rebuilt from scratch or polished.

02 — build

Concept from first principles

Every formula is derived on the board before it's used. Students who understand where an equation comes from stop panicking when a question rephrases it.

03 — practice

Guided problem sets

Weekly problem sets pulled from UNEB past papers 2010–2025, marked with the same scheme examiners use, so students see exactly where marks are lost.

04 — drill

Timed exam simulation

In the final term before UCE or UACE, sessions shift to full timed papers under exam conditions, followed by a mark-by-mark review.
